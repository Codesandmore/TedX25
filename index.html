About TEDx

TEDx events are independently organized gatherings designed to bring people together to share ideas and spark deep discussions. While TED focuses on a global scale, TEDx creates a space that highlights local perspectives, amplifying voices that drive change within communities. It has been a catalyst for spreading knowledge across the world, fostering a culture of innovation and dialogue. Licensed by TED, TEDx offers an engaging experience for speakers and attendees alike, providing a stage where local ideas gain global recognition. These talks are recorded and later shared for free, making knowledge accessible to all through TEDx platforms.

About TEDxMACE

TEDxMACE, organized at Mar Athanasius College of Engineering, Kothamangalam, serves as a transformative platform that connects individuals with a diverse spectrum of professionals from around the world. By hosting speakers from various vocations and fields of work, TEDxMACE provides invaluable international exposure, broadening perspectives and inspiring innovative thinking. Since its inception, it has been committed to fostering a culture of learning, collaboration, and intellectual growth, enabling people from all walks of life to engage with groundbreaking ideas and real-world experiences. Each edition continues to bridge the gap between different disciplines and industries, empowering a community of curious minds to explore new possibilities and drive meaningful impact.

Explore The Theme

At TEDxMACE 2025, we proudly present our theme for this year: "Curating Curiosity." What if the questions we ask are just as important as the answers we seek? Curiosity is the silent force behind every breakthrough, every leap into the unknown—it dares us to challenge norms, embrace uncertainty, and reimagine possibilities. "Curating Curiosity" is not just about celebrating the sparks of wonder; it’s about unraveling the limitless stories they ignite—tales of defiance, discovery, and transformation. Join us on an inspiring journey into the minds of those who didn’t just ask why but why not—visionaries who turned curiosity into a catalyst for change. Because sometimes, the most extraordinary adventures begin with a single, bold question.
