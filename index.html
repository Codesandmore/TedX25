About TEDxMACE

TEDxMACE is a platform for bringing together innovative thinkers, visionaries, and changemakers at Mar Athanasius College of Engineering. The event showcases thought-provoking talks, discussions, and performances.

About TEDx

TEDx is a global program that brings the spirit of TED to local communities. Independently organized under the TED license, these events feature ideas worth spreading, presented by speakers from diverse fields.
